A LETTER FROM KERIN

To everyone who has ever felt Lagos before understanding it,

This city raised me in sound, in heat, in movement that never really stops. Every record I make is just me trying to hold onto a moment that was already disappearing.

If you find yourself here, you are not just listening — you are remembering with me.

— KERIN
